RageDance Lineage

RageDance is a practice of movement and presence developed in lived contexts of stress, danger, and healing. It belongs to no single tradition and replaces none.

It emerges from:

  • Street reality: environments where threat, unpredictability, and compression shape behavior.
  • Somatic wisdom: breath, fascia, rhythm, and pulse-wave recognition.
  • Embodied ethics: choosing not to harm when harm is possible.
  • Communal repair: returning to the circle after rupture.

RageDance rejects domination, spectacle, intimidation, and posturing.

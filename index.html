The Practice

RageDance is a small-space movement practice built on breath, precision, and honest feeling. It blends somatic check-ins, martial arcs, Qigong sway, and street rhythm into a clear, repeatable cycle.

The goal is not performance. The goal is return: to your body, to your breath, to a steadier way of carrying rage, fear, and grief.

The RageDance Cycle

  • Arrival & Breath
  • Body Check-In
  • Neck & Facial Tapping
  • Shoulder Circles
  • Qigong Sway + Latin Hip Integration
  • Twin Infinity Loops (right & left)
  • Elbow Arc Drills
  • Pulse Reflection & Exit

What RageDance Is

RageDance is:

  • A somatic discipline you can practice on an 18-inch mat.
  • A way to move anger, fear, and adrenaline without harm.
  • A trauma-aware practice: voluntary, non-coercive, invitational.
  • A Flower Road practice meant to live in the commons, not in a brand.

What It Is Not

  • Not a performance or competition.
  • Not a therapy replacement.
  • Not a product for sale.
  • Not a training tool for police, military, or surveillance uses.

Using This Work

RageDance (Flower Road Practice) is distinct from similarly-named commercial offerings and must remain in the non-commercial, trauma-aware, community-rooted commons.

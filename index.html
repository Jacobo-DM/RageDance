RageDance

A practice of movement and presence for staying human under pressure.

What is RageDance?

RageDance is a practice developed through lived experience: street-level danger, internal compression, and the long work of recalibrating presence. It is not derived from any single tradition, nor does it replace existing arts.

RageDance centers:

  • Pulse awareness — the body’s internal rhythm in motion and stillness.
  • Movement that restores presence instead of escalating threat.
  • Boundaries that protect without dominating.
  • The return to the circle after rupture.

What RageDance Is Not

  • Not a performance system.
  • Not a method for intimidation or posturing.
  • Not a path for accumulating followers or power.

Any use of RageDance to elevate the self, impress others, or undermine the safety of the circle breaks the lineage.

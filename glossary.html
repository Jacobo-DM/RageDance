RageDance Glossary

Working notes on language in RageDance practice.

Pulse
The felt rhythm of your own body in stillness and motion — heartbeat, breath, tension and release.
Presence
Being aware of yourself and your surroundings without shutting down, spacing out, or attacking.
Boundary
A clear, embodied signal that says, “This is as close as you may come,” with the option to step away.

More terms will be added as the practice evolves.

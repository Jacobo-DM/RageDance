Ethics of RageDance Practice

Working draft. This page will hold clear agreements for anyone practicing or teaching RageDance.

  • Practice to stay human, not to dominate.
  • Never use RageDance to intimidate, posture, or humiliate.
  • Respect each person’s boundaries and nervous system.
  • Stop immediately if someone says “stop.”
  • Do not record or share others’ practice without explicit consent.

More will be added as the circle grows.
